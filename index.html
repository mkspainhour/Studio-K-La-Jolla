Welcome to Studio K La Jolla

Owned and operated by Kurt M Kikuchi, Studio K La Jolla is located in beautiful coastal La Jolla, California. Studio K La Jolla is a movement and wellness studio that specializes in Pilates, the GYROTONIC Method and massage therapy. Focusing on the art of mindful movement, Studio K La Jolla helps the individual find the most effective way to improve their flexibility, strength and overall body awareness.
